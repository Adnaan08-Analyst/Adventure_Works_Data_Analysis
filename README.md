# Adventure-Works-Data-Analysis
To explore Adventure Works datasets to uncover key performance metrics, optimize sales insights, and present data-driven findings through clear, interactive visualizations.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Adnaan08-Analyst/Adventure_Works_Data_Analysis/blob/main/Adventure%20works%20merged.xlsx"</a>

## Questions 
- What is the total revenue generated across different products and regions?
- Which product categories show the highest sales and profit margins?
- How does customer purchasing behavior vary by segment and time period?
- What is the average order value and how does it trend over time?
- Which sales channels contribute most to overall revenue?
- How efficiently are top-performing products converting into repeat purchases?
- What is the monthly and yearly sales growth rate?
- Which regions or territories show the highest sales performance?
- How does discounting impact revenue and profit?
- What are the top KPIs influencing overall business performance?
